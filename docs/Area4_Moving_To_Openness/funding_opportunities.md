See section 3.6.1 at this [link](https://nspires.nasaprs.com/external/viewrepositorydocument?cmdocumentid=860804&solicitationId=%7BC669B5EF-ACBB-A0E4-B57D-06F31DEABDB5%7D&viewSolicitationDocument=1)
     - Events focused on SMD data, software, or open science practices
